Here is some information you may not know about FestiveHomes.ie!

Welcome to FestiveHomes.ie!

We have an amazing selection of Christmas trees which are both real and artificial, leaving our customers with plenty to choose from!

Our company originated in Co. Kilkenny in 1938, and has been passed down through two generations. We have recently been awarded with the 'Best Customer Service Award 2015'

FestiveHomes originally focused on selling Christmas trees, but we are proud to announce that we have begun to sell Christmas as of this year. We our the first Kilkenny based company to provide the service of selling, delivering and decorating our trees!
